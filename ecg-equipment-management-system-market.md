<p><p>The ECG Equipment & Management System market encompasses various types of devices and systems used for cardiac monitoring. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG recording over 24-48 hours. Resting ECG systems capture heart activity in a stable state. Stress ECG monitors evaluate heart performance under physical stress. Event monitoring systems record sporadic heart events for later analysis. ECG management systems facilitate the storage, retrieval, and analysis of ECG data. Other devices may include portable monitors or specialized diagnostic tools, catering to diverse cardiac care needs.</p></p>
