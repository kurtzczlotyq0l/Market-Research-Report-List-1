<p><p>The Geophysical Services Market encompasses two primary survey types: aerial-based and land-based. Aerial-based surveys utilize aircraft or drones to collect geophysical data over large areas, offering rapid, high-resolution insights into geological features. In contrast, land-based surveys involve ground-level exploration techniques, such as seismic and electromagnetic methods, to analyze subsurface conditions and provide detailed local data. Both methods play crucial roles in resource exploration, environmental assessment, and infrastructure development, providing vital information for decision-making in various industries.</p></p>
